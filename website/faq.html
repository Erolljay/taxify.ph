Frequently asked questions

Can't find your answer? Write to hello@txform.ph.

Do I need Manager.io to use Txform?

No. Txform runs on a built-in accounting system based on Manager. The books live inside Txform — you don't need to buy or set up anything separately.

Which BIR forms and schedules are supported?

SLS, SLP, SAWT, QAP, Alphalist, plus BIR Forms 2307 and 2316 — with real-time VAT, Percentage Tax, EWT and Withholding Tax on Compensation reporting behind them, and native Income Statement and Balance Sheet.

How does batch import work?

You upload the sales, purchase, receivable, payable, or payroll spreadsheet you already keep. Txform reads it and books the entries into the accounting system for you — no manual re-keying.

What is the compliance wizard?

A guided flow that takes each return from filing, to payment, to recording — including generating the DAT file ready for eBIRForms / eFPS — so nothing falls through the cracks between steps.

How does pricing work for multiple clients?

A flat ₱500 per business per month. Each business you manage is billed separately, so you only pay for the clients you actually run in Txform.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is monthly per business — stop a business and billing for it stops at the end of the period. No lock-in.

Is my clients' data safe?

Connections are encrypted over HTTPS, data is backed up on a daily/weekly/monthly schedule, and we handle personal data in line with the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). See our Security and Privacy Policy.

How do I sign in?

Txform uses passwordless magic-link sign-in: enter your email and we send you a secure one-time link. No password to remember or leak.
