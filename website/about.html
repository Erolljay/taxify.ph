About

We built the tool we wished we had.

Txform started inside a working Philippine CPA firm — not a startup boardroom. It's the answer to our own filing season.

Built by people who file, not just people who code

Every quarter, the same grind: pull the numbers out of Excel, re-key them into the books, wrestle the schedules into shape, and race the BIR deadline across every client. We knew there had to be a better way — so we built it, first for ourselves.

Txform is that tool, hardened into a product. It takes the spreadsheets bookkeepers already keep and carries them all the way to BIR-ready filings — batch import, a File-Pay-Record wizard, real-time tax reports, and the forms your clients ask for. Because we run it on our own clients, it has to actually work. That's the bar.

What we believe

  • Meet people where they are. Bookkeepers live in Excel. Txform works with that reality instead of fighting it.
  • Compliance is a workflow, not a form. Filing, paying, and recording belong together — so we built them that way.
  • Data is a responsibility. As CPAs, confidentiality is in our DNA. See our Security and Privacy pages.

Who we are

Txform is built by TalloCPA, a certified public accounting practice based in Iloilo City, Philippines, led by Erol Jay Tallo, CPA. We built Txform to handle our own clients' BIR filings first — which is exactly why it has to work.
