Security & data privacy

Your clients' numbers, treated like our own.

We're a CPA firm before we're a software company. We built Txform knowing exactly what it means to hold a client's books — so protecting that data isn't a checkbox, it's the point.

Encrypted in transit

Every connection to Txform runs over HTTPS with modern TLS. Your uploads and reports never travel in the clear.

Backed up on a schedule

Automated daily, weekly, and monthly backups with defined retention, so a client's books are never a single point of failure.

Restricted access

Access to production data is limited and controlled. Your data is used to run the Service — never sold, never used for ads.

RA 10173 aligned

Built around the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 and NPC guidance. Full detail is in our Privacy Policy.

Passwordless sign-in

Access is via secure magic-link email — there's no reused password to leak or guess.

Data you can take with you

Your accounting data is yours. You can export it, and on account closure it's securely deleted per our retention rules.

Have a security question or need details for a client's due-diligence review? Write to privacy@txform.ph.
