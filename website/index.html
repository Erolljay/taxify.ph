Questions

Answers before you ask.

Do I need Manager.io to use Txform?

Txform runs on a built-in accounting system based on Manager. You don't need to buy or set up anything separately — the books live inside Txform.

Which BIR forms and schedules are supported?

SLS, SLP, SAWT, QAP, Alphalist, plus BIR Forms 2307 and 2316 — with real-time VAT, Percentage Tax, EWT and Withholding Tax on Compensation reporting behind them.

Is my clients' data safe?

Connections are encrypted over HTTPS, data is backed up daily/weekly/monthly, and we handle personal data in line with the Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). See our Security and Privacy Policy pages.

How does pricing work for multiple clients?

A flat ₱500 per business per month. Each business you manage is billed separately, so you only pay for the clients you actually run in Txform.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is monthly per business — stop a business and billing for it stops. No lock-in.
