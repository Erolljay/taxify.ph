💸 Batch Collect — Payables
Download open Purchase Invoices, fill in Payment Date / Bank Account / Amount Paid, then post Payments directly to Manager
ℹ️ "Download Open Invoices" pulls every Purchase Invoice that isn't fully paid as of today, one row per invoice. Fill in only the Payment Date, Bank/Cash Account, and Amount Paid columns for whichever invoices actually got paid — leave a row blank if it hasn't been paid yet. Re-upload the same file, click Validate, then Post to Manager to record a Payment against each matching invoice. Don't edit the Invoice ID, Invoice Date, Supplier, Reference, Invoice Amount, or Balance Due columns — those are used to match each row back to its invoice and aren't sent to Manager.
